About SACVPN

Why We Created SACVPN - Your Trusted VPN Service Provider

SACVPN exists to make private, fast, and secure internet access available to everyone - individuals, gamers, and businesses. We are builders and network engineers who were tired of bloated VPNs, confusing pricing, and weak performance. So we designed a secure VPN service powered by WireGuard technology that is simple, honest, and engineered for maximum speed and privacy protection.

Our story

SACVPN started as a practical response to three problems we kept seeing: 1) everyday people have their data collected, profiled, and sold without meaningful consent; 2) many VPNs make bold promises but deliver inconsistent speeds, limits on devices, and dark patterns during checkout; 3) businesses want simple, predictable security for remote work without standing up full enterprise stacks. We believed there was room for a VPN that respected users and treated performance as a first-class requirement.

We chose WireGuard from day one. It is modern, minimal, and efficient. By building around WireGuard and avoiding legacy protocols unless absolutely required, we can keep latency low, throughput high, and configuration simple. The result is a service where setup is measured in seconds, not afternoons.

What we are building

  • Personal and Gaming plans with unlimited devices so a whole household can be protected.
  • Gaming-optimized routes that favor lower latency and stability during peak hours.
  • Business tiers with clear device quotas and a simple management dashboard.
  • One-click device setup: generate a config, scan a QR code, and you are online.
  • No-logs policy enforced by design and process. We do not record browsing activity.
  • Infrastructure that prioritizes speed, minimal overhead, and regular updates.

The problem we are solving

  • ISPs throttle or shape certain traffic. A VPN prevents easy classification and slows throttling.
  • Public and shared networks expose devices to snooping and credential theft.
  • Smart TVs, consoles, and IoT devices leak metadata that can be tied to a household.
  • Remote work often mixes personal and business devices without a clean separation.
  • Many VPNs add limits, upsells, and friction that discourage consistent protection.

Why people need a VPN at home and in public

At home

  • Prevent your ISP from collecting and selling your browsing patterns.
  • Reduce throttling on streaming and cloud gaming during busy hours.
  • Protect every device - phones, tablets, PCs, TVs, and IoT.
  • Unlock additional streaming libraries when you travel.
  • Mask your household IP to reduce targeted advertising.
  • Keep financial activity private while shopping or banking.

On public networks

  • Encrypt traffic on cafe, hotel, airplane, and campus Wi-Fi.
  • Block trivial eavesdropping and credential interception.
  • Prevent captive portals and intermediaries from profiling your activity.
  • Maintain a consistent identity for apps that dislike IP changes.
  • Connect faster with lightweight WireGuard even on slower links.
