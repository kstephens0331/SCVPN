## Key Decisions Made

1. ✅ **Business 100 = Business 500 pricing** ($911.11/mo)
   - Both use 1 server
   - Business 500 offers better value ($1.82/device vs $9.11/device)
   - Incentivizes customers to scale up

2. ✅ **Uniform discount structure** (10%, 18.2%, 25%, 30.8%)
   - Applies to ALL plans (consumer and business)
   - Simple to understand and communicate
   - Maintains target margins at each tier

3. ✅ **Guaranteed 35% margin on 3-year plans**
   - All business plans profitable at 3-year commitment
   - No loss leaders
   - Sustainable long-term growth

4. ✅ **Server capacity at 75% max load**
   - Ensures performance and stability
   - Room for traffic spikes
   - Better customer experience than overselling
